Electric cars.

Everyone's talking about them.

But precious few are actually taking them out for a test drive.

Sure they save the environment, but they're just not fun, right?

 

This Digital Campaign will show that electric cars are fun.

 

The EcoRaces is a city-based ownership game, which encourages DriveNow users to try out the new BMW i3 for themselves in an attempt to control the city through eco-friendly driving.

Website

This will be the informational hub. It will provide participants and viewers the chance to see current news from the city, such as turf ownership, as well as registration forms.

i3 Navigation Screen

Using an enhanced DriveNow and i3 smart navigation system we will enable players to view their efficiency, their scores, and a map of the territories.

DriveNow App

The current DriveNow app will be modified to allow players to keep track of their score and their team's territory. The app will allow them to receive notifications, recruit more drivers, and spread the word through social media.
