It's lonely getting older. You can all of a sudden feel like a burden and out of place in a community that you used to be such a part of. It even has negative health implications for these lonely seniors

 

Airbnb is in a perfect position to bring these seniors back into society. Working with ageUK, we will connect them with travelers, allowing them to share their stories and making them feel a sense of purpose and belonging again.

 

CW: Javed Jasani

AD: Evgeniya Marukhina

Tutor: Said Fayad, Freelance AD

 

Airbnb and ageUK will work together and find seniors who wish to become storytellers. During the booking process, travelers will not only see rooms for rent, but also the location and profiles of storytellers in that city.

Each storyteller will have a profile page, where they can talk about themselves and their history, as well as signify whether their available for tours, or to meet up and chat.

Step by step, with Airbnb forming partnerships globally, we can give purpose back to the eldery and make both travelers and seniors feel like they belong.
